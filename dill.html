Season for growing Dill------ Dill is a cool-season herb that thrives in moderate temperatures. It can be grown from spring to late summer, with the optimal time for sowing seeds being in the spring after the last frost. In warmer climates like Zones 9 and 10, dill can be grown from fall until late spring according to a YouTube video.
