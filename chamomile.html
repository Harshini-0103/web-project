Season for growing Chamomile-------The best time to grow chamomile is during the cool season, specifically spring and fall. Chamomile thrives in cooler temperatures and is sensitive to extreme heat according to gardening websites and videos. In mild winter climates, you can start seeds indoors in late summer for fall planting, or directly outdoors in spring. In colder climates, spring planting is recommended after the last frost experts advise.
