Benefites of Chive

1.Rich in Nutrients: Chives are a low-calorie source of essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamins A, C, and K, as well as potassium, calcium, and iron.They also contain choline and folate.
2.Antioxidant Power: Loaded with antioxidants like flavonoids and sulfur compounds, chives help protect cells from damage caused by free radicals, potentially reducing the risk of chronic diseases.
3. Potential Anti-Cancer Properties: Studies suggest that compounds in chives, particularly organosulfur compounds, may have anti-cancer effects by slowing the growth of cancer cells or inhibiting their spread, according to Lybrate.
4.Supports Bone Health: Chives contain vitamin K, crucial for maintaining strong bones and potentially reducing the risk of osteoporosis.
5.Aids in Digestion: Chives offer dietary fiber, which promotes healthy digestion and can help with bloating and constipation. They also possess mild antibacterial properties that might help keep harmful gut bacteria in check.
